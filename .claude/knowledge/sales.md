## ICP playbook

- **Primary ICP — Port agents and freight forwarders.** Pain: 3–21 day SWIFT settlement on cross-border invoices. Hook: get paid in minutes, no SWIFT delays, no customer wallet required.
- **Secondary ICP — Ship owner finance teams / mid-market carrier CFOs.** Pain: paying hundreds of port agents globally, FX surprises, working capital tied up. Hook: cash conversion cycle from days to minutes, audit-ready ledger, ERP integration.

## Discovery call template

_(Tighten over time. Each agent invocation can update.)_

1. How do you currently get paid by [counterparty type]?
2. What's your typical settlement time today, end-to-end?
3. What does a delayed payment cost you operationally?
4. Who else inside your company touches a single payment (finance, ops, legal)?
5. What would have to be true for you to switch payment rails?

## Objections playbook

_(Patterns to watch for. Add as new objections come up.)_

- **"Crypto sounds risky"** → reframe as stablecoin / payment rail; emphasize Circle as regulated USDC issuer; offer references
- **"My customer won't have a wallet"** → ship payer-fiat-pay (in roadmap, P0); meanwhile Privy-managed wallets remove the wallet UX entirely
- **"What about regulation in [my country]?"** → defer to compliance agent for specifics; default position is "we operate in EU and LATAM today"
- **"Pricing"** → start with Growth tier; volume discounts available; pilot first to prove value
